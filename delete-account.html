How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your TRUK Africa account in two ways:

Method 1: Delete via Mobile App (Recommended)

  1. Open the TRUK Africa app on your device
  2. Go to your Profile screen
  3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account" button
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Select a reason for deletion (optional)
  6. Confirm your decision
  7. Your account will be scheduled for deletion

Note: Your account will be permanently deleted after 30 days. You can cancel the deletion within this period by logging back in.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

  1. Send an email to hello@trukafrica.com
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email or phone number
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

⚠️ Important Information

What happens when you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information will be removed
  • Active bookings will be cancelled (if applicable)
  • You will lose access to your account immediately
  • Some data may be retained for legal/compliance purposes (as per our Privacy Policy)

Data Retention: We may retain certain information for up to 3 years after account deletion for legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution purposes, as required by Kenyan law.
