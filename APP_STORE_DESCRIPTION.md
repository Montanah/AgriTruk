## Full Description

**TRUKapp** is a comprehensive logistics and transportation platform designed to revolutionize how goods move from farm to market and beyond. Whether you're shipping agricultural products, general cargo, or managing a transportation fleet, TRUKapp connects you with the right transporters and provides powerful tools to manage your logistics operations efficiently.

### **For Shippers & Businesses**

**Move Your Goods with Ease**
- **agriTRUK**: Specialized service for transporting farm goods, produce, and all types of agricultural products from farm to market
- **cargoTRUK**: General cargo and logistics transportation for any type of goods
- **Instant Booking**: Create service requests and connect with verified transporters instantly
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor your shipments with live GPS tracking and delivery updates
- **Consolidation Services**: Optimize costs by consolidating multiple shipments
- **Transparent Pricing**: Get upfront cost estimates before booking
- **Secure Payments**: Safe and secure payment processing

**Perfect for:**
- Farmers and agricultural producers
- Businesses needing regular cargo transport
- Individuals moving goods or luggage
- Corporate logistics departments

### **For Transporters**

**Manage Your Fleet Like Never Before**

**Individual Transporters:**
- Accept and manage booking requests
- Track your earnings and performance
- Build your reputation with customer ratings
- Access job opportunities in your area
- Manage multiple vehicles

**Company Transporters:**
- **Fleet Management**: Complete control over your vehicle fleet
- **Driver Management**: Assign drivers, track performance, and manage schedules
- **Route Optimization**: Plan efficient routes and maximize load capacity
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Real-time insights into fleet performance, revenue, and utilization
- **Driver Recruitment**: Access a marketplace of qualified job seekers
- **Maintenance Tracking**: Keep your fleet in optimal condition
- **Comprehensive Reports**: Detailed reports on operations, revenue, and driver performance

**Subscription Plans Available:**
- Basic: Perfect for individual transporters (up to 3 vehicles)
- Pro: For growing operations (up to 10 vehicles, advanced analytics)
- Enterprise: Unlimited vehicles with custom integrations and dedicated support

### **For Brokers**

**Connect the Logistics Ecosystem**
- Connect businesses, shippers, and transporters
- Manage multiple client relationships
- Track all your transactions in one place
- Build your network and expand your business
- Access performance analytics
- Subscription-based access with flexible plans

### **For Drivers**

**Your Job Management Hub**
- View assigned jobs and routes
- Real-time navigation and trip guidance
- Update job status and delivery confirmations
- Track your performance and earnings
- Communicate with dispatchers and customers
- Access traffic and route information

### **Key Features**

✅ **Live GPS Tracking** - Real-time location updates for all shipments
